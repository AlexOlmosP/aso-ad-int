# Keyword Research Depth Skill

## Methodology (PICKASO 5-Step Process)
1. **Investigate**: Brainstorm all keywords related to the app's function, category, and user intent
2. **Prioritize**: Analyze by traffic volume, difficulty, and relevance
3. **Optimize**: Place keywords in highest-weight fields (title > subtitle > description)
4. **Monitor**: Track position changes over time
5. **Repeat**: Keywords are volatile — continuously update

## Signal Sources for Suggestions
- **Store Autocomplete**: Both Google Play suggest and App Store hints — position in suggestions indicates popularity
- **Competitor Titles**: Extract keywords from top-ranking competitor app titles
- **Semantic Expansion**: Find related terms, synonyms, and long-tail variants
- **Google Search Suggest**: Cross-reference with web search autocomplete for demand validation
- **Category Keywords**: Terms that define the app's category and DNA

## Scoring Criteria
- **Volume**: Estimated search traffic (0-100 scale)
- **Difficulty**: Based on competition intensity — how many top apps target this keyword
  - Low: <3 of top 10 have keyword in title
  - Medium: 3-5 of top 10 have keyword in title
  - High: >5 of top 10 have keyword in title

## Best Practices
- Prioritize mid/long-tail keywords for new apps (lower difficulty)
- Include both generic and brand-adjacent terms
- Consider localization — keywords vary by country
- Avoid over-optimizing for single high-volume keywords
- Balance between short-tail (high volume, high competition) and long-tail (lower volume, easier to rank)
